Synopsis

Tensai Shitara slime datta Ken(I reincarnated as a slime) is a story about a salaryman who is murdered and reincarnates in a sword and sorcery world as a slime with unique powers and gathers allies to build his own nation of monsters.

characters

Rimuru Tempest
Rimuru Tempest is the main character of the show. He has unique sets of magic powers which makes him very powerful in view of the fact that he is just a mere slime.
Benimaru
Benimaru is an assistant of Rimuru. He possesses incredible sword skills which enables him to be strong.
Shuna
Shuna is another assistant of Rimuru. She possesses incredible weaving skills and magic powers.
Gobta
Gotba is one of Rimuru's first encounter. He is a hobgoblin that helps out around the village and assists Rimuru in warfares.
Shion
Shion is a powerful secretary of Rimuru. She possesses incredible power but is sometimes too pushy.
